Urban Goddess NYC Event

The Urban Goddess is a creative, collaborative, loving series of different presentations and activities designed to empower, awaken and give you tools to live a happier and healthier life. The idea behind the poster was to use the colors of the top three Chakra's in the body, the throat(blue), the third eye(indigo) and the crown(violet). The image was supplied by the client.
